My favorite Cartoon character

Shinchan

About my favourite cartoon character

Shinchan Nohara is a 5 year old boy who lives in kasukabe , with his family and friends . He has a cute little sister named Himawari Nohara . His mother's name is Mitsy Nohara and his father's name is Hiroshi Nohara . They also have a pet dog named shiro . They still have to pay 32 years mortgage of their house.

I like shinchan because he is brave and fearless boy . I also like his mother as she is a hardworking lady and always handles two kids easily . even his sister himawari is brave like shinchan and naughty too ! His father Hiroshi is a salesman in action sales department .
